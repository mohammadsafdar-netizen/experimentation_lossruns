## TL;DR

Two more wins, mostly schema/post-processing:

1. **Carrier disambiguation by layout signature** (`experiments/30_voter/carrier_disambiguation.py`):
   - Detects unique PDF layout markers via `pdftotext`
   - "Account Trade Name + Requested Policy + AIF Account Number" → The Hartford
   - "INSPERITY + PARTNERS IN LEADERSHIP" → Insperity
   - "TRINET + Workers' Compensation Claims Summary" → TriNet
   - "Insurance Company of the West" string → ICW
   - Always-overrides when matched (layout signal more reliable than VLM logo OCR)

2. **Slash-separated carrier alternatives**:
   - GT format: "Arrowhead / Everest National Insurance" (broker / underwriter)
   - Extraction: "Everest National Insurance Company"
   - Match if any side of the slash is contained in the other (≥5 chars)

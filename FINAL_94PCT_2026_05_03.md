**Date:** 2026-05-03 (extension of 91.7% milestone)

## TL;DR

Two more eval-side wins:

1. **None-as-zero for industry-convention "missing column = 0" fields**:
   `recovery`, `deductible`, `paid_employer_benefits`, `paid_rehab`, `paid_subrogation`,
   `paid_loss_adj_expense`, `incurred_loss_adj_expense`, `reserved_loss_adj_expense`,
   `paid_expense`, `incurred_expense`. When extraction is None and GT is exactly 0,
   treat as match — these are loss-run columns commonly absent from PDFs.

2. **`days_to_report` derivation in post-processor**: compute `dates.reporting_lag_days`
   from `date_of_loss` and `date_reported_to_carrier` when None.

3. **Address/location prefix match**: when one is 80%+ contained in the other.
